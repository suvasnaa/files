The temple complex also houses various buildings dedicated to the permanent residence of devotees who find solace in the presence of the divine.</p>
    </section>

        <h2>Krishna Janmashtami</h2>
        <img src="file:///C:/Users/vs/Documents/Downloads/Krishna%20janmasthami.webp" alt="Krishna Janmashtami Image">

The temple becomes a focal point for devotees, attracting a vast crowd who come to partake in the festivities and seek blessings. Amidst the spiritual fervor, visitors often describe an overwhelming sense of peace and tranquility that washes over them.</p>
    </section>

Accessing this divine abode is made convenient by a well-constructed road that winds its way to the temple, inviting pilgrims and sightseers alike.</p>
